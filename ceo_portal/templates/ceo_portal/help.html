Executive Overview

Welcome to the ARK Executive Command Center manual. This documentation outlines the operational protocols, intelligence gathering mechanics, and vault management procedures available exclusively to the CEO. Misuse or unauthorized distribution of this intelligence is strictly prohibited.

Read Carefully Before Operating The Console

The Vault (Inventory)

  • Adding Assets: Use the "Deploy New Asset" or "Mass Deployment" functions to add vehicles to the vault. Ensure high-quality imagery and accurate SKUs.

  • Availability Toggle: Assets can be marked as "Secured" (Hidden from public catalog) or "Available" instantly via the toggle switch in the Vault list.

  • Mass Purge: Select multiple assets and use the Mass Purge feature to permanently delete outdated inventory.

Intelligence (Leads)

  • Lead Tracking: All incoming inquiries from the public portal are routed here. They contain contact info and the requested asset details.

  • Client Dossiers: For high-priority leads, generate a Printable Dossier to take into meetings or pass to associates.

Sourcing Desk

  • Custom Requests: Manage bespoke requests from VIP clients looking for assets not currently in the vault.

  • Status Management: Update the status of a request from "Pending Review" to "In Progress" or "Fulfilled".

  • Broker Notes: Add internal encrypted notes to a sourcing request to track negotiations and broker interactions.

Threat Console

  • Decoy Admin Logs: The system automatically logs IP addresses and user agents of anyone attempting to breach the decoy `/admin` portal.

  • Threat Resolution: Review logs and mark threats as "Resolved" once investigated. Unresolved threats flash on the sidebar.

  • System Sanitization: Use the "Purge Resolved" button to permanently delete addressed logs and keep the database clean.
